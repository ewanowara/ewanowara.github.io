Ewa Nowara

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Joohns Hopkins University where I work with Prof. Rama Chellappa, on computer vision and deep learning. I am currently working on geo-localizing natural images. I received my Ph.D. from Rice University in 2021 where I worked on imaging vital signs from webcam video recordings.
